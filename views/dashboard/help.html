Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about using Pinnacle e-Invoice Solution LHDN Middleware.

Can I customize invoice templates?

Yes, Pinnacle e-Invoice Solution LHDN Middleware offers multiple invoice templates that you can customize. You can modify colors, add your logo, and adjust layouts through the Settings > Invoice Settings menu. (This feature is in experimental stage and under active development)

How do I generate reports?

Navigate to the Inbound page in the dashboard or top navigation bar. Select your desired invoice type, set the date range and filters, then click "View Details". Reports can be exported in PDF but we will support more formats soon such as Excel, or CSV formats.

How do I add multiple users?

Administrators can add users through the Settings > User Management menu. Each user can be assigned specific roles and permissions to control their access levels.

Is my data secure?

Yes, Pinnacle e-Invoice Solution LHDN Middleware employs industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure data centers, and regular backups. We comply with data protection regulations and maintain strict privacy policies.
